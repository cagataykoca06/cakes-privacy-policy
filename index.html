Privacy Policy

Data We Collect

We collect your name, birthday (month and day only — no year), and favourite cake type to personalise your experience. Room and event data you create is stored securely in Firebase Firestore.

Your data is used solely to provide the Cakes app experience. We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for advertising purposes.

Data Storage & Security

All data is stored in Google Firebase (Firestore and Authentication), protected by Firebase Security Rules that restrict access to your own account only.

Data Retention

You can delete your account at any time from Settings → Delete Account. This permanently removes all your data from our servers.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time through the app. For additional requests, contact us at birthday.cakes.app@gmail.com.
